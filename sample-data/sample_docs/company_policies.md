# Company Policies

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Last Revised:** September 15, 2024

---

## 1. Return Policy

All hardware and accessory products purchased directly from us may be returned within **30 calendar days** of the delivery date, subject to the following conditions:

- **Condition:** Items must be in their original packaging, with all included accessories, cables, and documentation. Products showing signs of physical damage, modification, or excessive wear beyond normal inspection are not eligible for return.
- **Proof of Purchase:** A valid order confirmation email or invoice number is required to initiate any return.
- **Non-Returnable Items:** Software licenses, subscription services, consulting engagements, and custom-configured hardware (e.g., servers built to specification) are not eligible for return once activated or delivered.
- **Process:** Contact our support team at returns@example.com or submit a return request through your account dashboard. You will receive a prepaid shipping label within 2 business days. Once the item is received and inspected at our warehouse, the return will be processed.
- **Restocking Fee:** Returns initiated after 15 days but within the 30-day window are subject to a 10% restocking fee, deducted from the refund amount.

### Exceptions

Enterprise customers with an active Premium Support Plan may negotiate extended return windows as part of their service agreement. Contact your assigned account manager for details.

---

## 2. Refund Policy

Approved returns are refunded to the original payment method within **5-10 business days** of warehouse receipt and inspection.

- **Credit Card / Debit Card:** Refunds appear on your statement within 5-10 business days after processing. Your issuing bank may take additional time to reflect the credit.
- **Wire Transfer / ACH:** Refunds are issued via the same method and typically arrive within 7-10 business days.
- **Store Credit:** If preferred, customers may opt for store credit instead of a monetary refund. Store credit is issued at 105% of the refund value as a goodwill incentive and does not expire.
- **Partial Refunds:** If a returned item is missing components or shows damage not disclosed at the time of return request, a partial refund may be issued at our discretion. The customer will be notified and given the option to have the item shipped back at their expense instead.
- **Subscription Refunds:** Monthly subscriptions may be cancelled at any time; no refund is issued for the current billing period. Annual subscriptions cancelled within the first 30 days receive a full refund. After 30 days, the remaining term is converted to store credit on a prorated basis.

## 3. Warranty

All hardware products carry a **standard 1-year limited warranty** from the date of purchase covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

### What Is Covered

- Manufacturing defects (e.g., dead pixels exceeding tolerance, non-functional ports, premature component failure)
- Firmware or embedded software issues present at time of manufacture
- Battery degradation below 80% of rated capacity within the warranty period (portable devices only)

### What Is Not Covered

- Damage resulting from misuse, accidents, unauthorized modification, or use outside documented operating conditions (temperature, humidity, voltage)
- Normal wear and tear, including cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality
- Damage caused by third-party accessories or software not approved by us
- Products with removed or altered serial number labels

### Extended Warranty

Customers may purchase an **Extended Warranty** within 60 days of the original purchase:

| Plan           | Duration       | Coverage                                   | Price            |
|----------------|----------------|--------------------------------------------|------------------|
| Standard+      | 2 years total  | Same as standard warranty                  | 10% of item cost |
| Premium Care   | 3 years total  | Standard + accidental damage (1 incident)  | 18% of item cost |
| Enterprise SLA | 3 years total  | Premium Care + next-business-day on-site replacement | Custom quote |

Warranty claims are initiated through the support portal. Approved claims are fulfilled via repair or replacement at our discretion. If the original product is no longer available, a comparable or superior model will be provided.

## 4. Data Handling and Customer Privacy

We are committed to protecting customer data in accordance with applicable privacy regulations, including GDPR and CCPA where applicable.

### Data We Collect

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, company name, billing and shipping addresses, phone number.
- **Transaction Data:** Order history, payment method (last four digits only; full card numbers are never stored on our systems), shipping records.
- **Usage Data:** For software and subscription products, anonymized usage telemetry (feature usage frequency, error reports) collected with explicit opt-in consent. Customers may disable telemetry at any time from their account settings.
- **Support Interactions:** Records of support tickets, chat transcripts, and phone call summaries for quality assurance and training.

### Data Retention

- Active customer data is retained for the duration of the business relationship plus **3 years** after the last transaction or account activity.
- After the retention period, data is anonymized or permanently deleted within 90 days.
- Customers may request early deletion by contacting privacy@example.com. Requests are fulfilled within 30 days, subject to legal retention obligations (e.g., tax records).

### Data Sharing

Customer data is never sold to third parties. Data may be shared with:

- **Payment processors** for transaction fulfillment (PCI-DSS compliant partners only)
- **Shipping carriers** for order delivery (name and address only)
- **Law enforcement** when required by valid legal process

### Security Measures

- All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3.
- Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.
- Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis, with audit logging on all access events.
- We conduct annual third-party security audits and penetration testing. Summary results are available to Enterprise customers upon request under NDA.
